Our Mission

Driven by an unwavering commitment to extend quality healthcare to underserved and remote regions of India, Shan Group proudly presents its state-of-the-art 100-bed multi-specialty hospital in District Amroha, Uttar Pradesh.

Shan Multi Speciality Hospital is a newly established, modern healthcare facility, dedicated to delivering compassionate, high-quality medical care with advanced infrastructure and a collaborative environment. The hospital is equipped with facilities like X-Ray, pathology lab, dialysis, CT scan, MRI, Physiotherapy, OT, ICU, Ultrasound, dental and ophthalmology, etc.

World-Class Medical Care

This advanced healthcare facility is designed to deliver world-class medical care through a blend of modern infrastructure, cutting-edge technology, and compassionate service. The hospital features advanced diagnostic laboratories, modern operation theatres, 24x7 emergency and OPD services, ensuring accessible, affordable, and reliable healthcare for individuals and families across the region.

A Vision for a Healthier Future

With a vision to make quality healthcare available to every corner of the country, this hospital marks the first milestone in Shan Group's larger healthcare mission — to establish a chain of modern, multi-specialty hospitals across India, particularly focusing on rural and remote communities.

Aligned with the vision of Dr. Shan Chaudhary, this initiative reflects the Group's enduring dedication to social welfare, compassion, and excellence in patient-centered care. The hospital is committed to serving the poor and needy, addressing critical health challenges, and improving the overall quality of life through ethical, affordable, and innovative healthcare solutions.

Beyond expanding its hospital network, Shan Group is actively exploring ways to extend its healthcare initiatives to improve medical services in remote and underserved regions of India. The Group aims to collaborate with healthcare professionals, NGOs, and government bodies to strengthen medical outreach, telemedicine support, and community wellness programs.
