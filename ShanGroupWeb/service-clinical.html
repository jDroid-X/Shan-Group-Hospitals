Core Clinical Care Services

Continuous and comprehensive primary and specialty medical care ensuring patients receive timely diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up under expert supervision.

24-Hour Patient Admission and OPD Services

Round-the-clock admission and outpatient services ensure uninterrupted access to medical care. Patients can seek consultation, follow-up treatment, and emergency attention at any hour. A structured triage system prioritizes urgent cases while maintaining efficient patient flow and documentation accuracy.

  • Seamless Admissions 24/7
  • Dedicated Triage Desk
  • Comfortable outpatient waiting areas

Specialized Cardiology & Orthopedics

Cardiology: Evaluation and management of heart-related conditions, including hypertension, coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart failure. Preventive cardiac screenings, ECG monitoring, and treatment planning are performed under specialist supervision.

Orthopedics: Our specialists diagnose and treat fractures, arthritis, sports injuries, spinal disorders, and joint problems. Treatment includes medication, physiotherapy guidance, and surgical intervention when required.

Gynecological and Obstetric Conditions

Comprehensive women's healthcare services include prenatal care, fertility evaluation, menstrual disorder management, menopause care, and high-risk pregnancy monitoring. Patient-centered counseling and evidence-based treatment ensure safety and comfort at every stage of reproductive health.

  • 24-hour normal & C-section delivery
  • High-risk pregnancy monitoring
  • Patient-centered reproductive health
