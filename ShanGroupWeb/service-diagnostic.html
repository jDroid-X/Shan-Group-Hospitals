Diagnostic & Laboratory Services

Deliver accurate and timely diagnostic evaluation. Support precise clinical decisions through advanced imaging and laboratory analysis.

MRI and CT Scan Facilities

Advanced MRI and CT scan systems offer detailed cross-sectional imaging for neurological, cardiac, abdominal, and musculoskeletal conditions. High-resolution imaging improves diagnostic precision, aiding early detection and effective treatment strategies.

  • Neuro & Cardiac Imaging
  • High diagnostic precision

Digital X-ray & Ultrasound

X-ray Imaging: Digital radiography systems provide fast and accurate imaging for fractures, chest conditions, and internal abnormalities. Radiologists analyze images promptly.

Ultrasound: Non-invasive and safe, this technology supports pregnancy monitoring, abdominal assessment, and soft tissue evaluation—enabling real-time visualization.

Advanced Pathology Laboratory

The pathology laboratory conducts blood tests, biochemical analysis, microbiology screening, and specialized diagnostics. Automated analyzers ensure accuracy and quick turnaround times, enabling physicians to make prompt and reliable treatment decisions.

  • Biochemical Analysis
  • Microbiology Screening
  • Quick Turnaround Times
