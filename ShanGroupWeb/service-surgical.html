Fully equipped operation theatres support general, orthopedic, gynecological, and specialty surgeries. Sterile environments, advanced anesthesia systems, and modern surgical instruments ensure patient safety. Strict infection control protocols and monitoring systems maintain high clinical standards.

Minimally Invasive (Laparoscopic) Procedures

Advanced laparoscopic procedures allow smaller incisions, reduced pain, and quicker recovery. When necessary, open surgeries are performed with precision and safety. Surgeons evaluate each case carefully to choose the most effective and least invasive approach possible.
